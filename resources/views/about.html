About UniEvent

Streamlining university event management for a better experience

Our Mission

To simplify and enhance the university event experience for everyone involved - from organizers to attendees.

UniEvent was created to address the challenges faced by universities in managing events, tracking attendance, and issuing certificates. Our platform brings together students, staff, and administrators in one seamless system.

Key Features:
  • Easy event registration and payment
  • QR code attendance tracking
  • Automated certificate generation
  • Real-time event analytics
  • Multi-role user management
